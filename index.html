Welcome to Alfa Foodline

We Provide High-Quality Raisins.

Central Asia has been a vital hub for cultural and economic exchange, fostering the development of trade and agriculture. Uzbekistan's unique climate is ideal for growing grape varieties like "Kishmish" and "Timur," used to produce renowned raisins such as Black Raisins and Sultana.

Natural Raisins

At Alfa Foodline, we use high-quality equipment for our raisin processing, ensuring our products maintain their natural sweetness and attractive appearance.

Quality Assurance

As a leading manufacturer in Uzbekistan, we are dedicated to meeting the highest standards, guaranteeing that our raisins are exceptional in taste and quality.

Our Service

What we do

Uzbekistan, with its sharply continental climate, is particularly well-suited for growing grape varieties such as "Kishmish," "White Kishmish," and "Timur.

Grape
Cultivation

We proudly cultivate grapes on our own plantations, carefully tending to every stage of growth.

Processing and
Drying Grapes

Our grapes undergo thorough sorting and processing. We select only the best berries to preserve their natural taste and aroma.

Export of
Our Raisins

We proudly export our products internationally, offering a natural product of the highest quality and taste.

Why Choose Us

Renowned for our reliability and commitment to quality, we consistently deliver exceptional products that meet the highest standards.

Scope

Our vineyards span over 150 hectares of the most fertile land in the Jambay district of Samarkand.

Excellent Taste

Boasting over 300 sunny days each year and average summer temperatures above 30 degrees Celsius, coupled with fertile soils, Uzbekistan produces large and sweet grapes.

High Quality

With our advanced facilities and commitment to excellence, Alfa Foodline continues to set the standard for premium raisin production in Uzbekistan and beyond.
