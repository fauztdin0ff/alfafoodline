Sultana (Ob Jush)

Our experienced agricultural specialists carefully monitor every stage of production to ensure exceptional quality of the product.

Description

Sultana raisins are very popular due to their wide range of possible applications in the food industry, that is, cooking, bakery, confectionery and cereal industries.

The texture of Sultana raisins is rich in nutrients, minerals and vitamins, so its regular use helps to improve all body functions. It is good to know that the constant consumption of this valuable nutrient is greatly beneficial to the health state of teeth and bones, as opposed to many other sugars and sweets, and is also useful for prevention of osteoporosis.

The grapes we use to produce the Sultana raisins are grown in our own vineyards of our farm, which is located in the Samarkand region of the Republic of Uzbekistan and is one of the most favorable locations to grow the grapes.

Our experienced agricultural specialists carefully monitor every stage of production to ensure exceptional quality of the product.

Sizes

  • Caliber 6+
  • 100 grams 270+320 pieces
  • Size 1cm - 2cm

Information about the product

  • Place of cultivation and production: Samarkand region, Republic of Uzbekistan Harvest time: August
  • Size: 5-8 mm
  • Humidity: 11 %
  • Shape: oval, semi-hollow texture, seedless
  • Color: light to dark brown
  • Taste: sweet, delicious, attributed to raisins, without flavors, slightly caramelized and sweet.
  • Smell: typical fragrance of Sultana without an foreign odor
  • Feedstock: made from grapes grown in the own vineyards of Alfa Foodline, treated with alkali before solar drying, does not contain GMOs and grown naturally.
  • Cleaning method: rinsing in clean water, electronic 4stage cleaning and sorting from foreign bodies, metal detection, laser-optical and X-ray methods
  • Sorting method: by color, size, structure and inclusions of stone, glass, metal and foreign matters
  • Purpose: for industrial use, raisins can also be consumed on its own, used for cooking various dishes, and as raw materials for other food products. Packaging: cardboard box with a 10 and 12.5 kg food grade polyethylene liner.
  • Delivery: by sea or by car container
  • Shelf life: twelve months from the date of production
